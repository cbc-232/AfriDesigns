Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to FAQs by clients who where in doubt have been provided. If this doesn't help? feel free to contact us.

How can I work with AfriDesigns?

You can get started by reaching out through our contact form to directly signup for our services. Once we understand your needs, we'll guide you through the process from design requirements to project delivery.

How much do you charge for your services?

Our pricing varies based on the scope and complexity of the service or product. contact us for a personalized quote that fits your specific needs and budget.

How will I receive my finished product or service?

Once your project is complete, we'll deliver it trough the method that works best for you- whether it's digital delivery, shipping of physical products, or in-person collection.

How can I pay for your services?

We offer payment options, including bank transfers, mobile payments, and online payment gateways. Full details will be provided when you receive your invoice.

When will my service or product be delivered?

Delivery times vary depending on the service or product requested. We will provide you with an estimated timeline based on your schedule when you place the order, and we'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer ongoing support after delivering the service or product?

Yes we provide ongoing support to ensure your satisfaction with our services. Whether it's technical assistance, updates, or futher customization, our team is here to help even after the project is completed.
